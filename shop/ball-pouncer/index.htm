Product Description

Ball Pouncer is 1st Tennis Ball Hopper Designed for Kids

The Ball Pouncer is a fun and new way for kids to pick up tennis balls. It is perfectly kid-sized, and extremely lightweight. At just 2 pounds, every child from age 3+ can pickup balls on the tennis court. It is designed to pickup traditional yellow tennis balls and Quick Start Red, Orange and Green Youth Tennis Balls. Now picking up the balls is actually fun!! Available in orange or aqua.

The Ball Pouncer was developed by tennis teaching pros (and brothers) Phillip, Steven and David Olesen.

Our goal was to develop a children’s tennis product that made picking up tennis balls much easier and a lot more FUN! Now not only is hitting the ball fun but picking them up is a blast as well!
